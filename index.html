About

I am a second-year Master's student in Sun Yat-sen University, China, supervised by Professor Chao Yu. I mainly focus on reinforcement learning (RL), especially under various settings, including offline, safe, and multi-objective scenarios. I hope to advance the real-world applications of RL by enhancing agents'generalization across diverse tasks and environments, while prioritizing their safety and robustness. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree in computer science from the same university.

I am expected to graduate in June 2025, and I intend to apply for a PhD program in the fall of 2025.
