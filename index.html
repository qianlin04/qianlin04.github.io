About

I am a third-year Master's student from the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University, China, supervised by Professor Chao Yu. I mainly focus on reinforcement learning (RL), especially under various settings, including offline, safe, and multi-objective scenarios. My goal is to advance the real-world applications of RL by enhancing agents'generalization across diverse tasks and environments, while prioritizing their safety and robustness.

I am expected to graduate in June 2025, and I intend to apply for a PhD program in the fall of 2025.
