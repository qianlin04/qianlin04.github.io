About

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the Kahlert School of Computing, advised by Dr. Daniel S. Brown. Previously, I earned my master’s degree from Sun Yat-sen University in China. My research focuses on reinforcement learning, with an emphasis on learning from offline data and human feedback, safe and constrained decision-making, and multi-objective optimization. More recently, I have been working on model interpretability in human feedback alignment systems.
